Tips to Enjoy your Tours More

Some of the best recommendations or advice for tourists around the world to know while they are coming to visit Ethiopia are: Check the visa requirements: Depending on your nationality, you may need a visa to enter Ethiopia. You can apply for an e-visa online or get a visa on arrival at Addis Ababa airport. Make sure you have a valid passport, a return ticket and enough money for your stay. Pack appropriately: Ethiopia has different climates and altitudes, so you should pack layers and warm clothes, especially if you plan to visit the highlands or the mountains. You should also bring sun protection, insect repellent, a first aid kit and any medications you may need. Be respectful of the culture and religion: Ethiopia is a diverse country with many ethnic groups and religions. You should respect the local customs and traditions, such as dressing modestly, greeting people properly, and avoiding public displays of affection. You should also be aware of the religious festivals and holidays, such as Orthodox Christian fasting days, when some restaurants and attractions may be closed or have limited services.Try the local food and drink: Ethiopia has a rich and varied cuisine that you should not miss. You can try dishes like injera (a sourdough flatbread), wat (a spicy stew), tibs (grilled meat), and shiro (a chickpea paste). You can also enjoy the famous Ethiopian coffee, which is often served in a traditional ceremony with incense and popcorn. Bargain wisely: Ethiopia is a relatively cheap country to travel in, but you should still bargain for goods and services, such as souvenirs, taxis, and tours. However, you should not be too aggressive or rude when bargaining, as this may offend the sellers or drivers. You should also be aware of the exchange rate and the local currency, which is the Ethiopian birr
